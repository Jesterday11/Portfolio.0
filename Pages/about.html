About Me

Howdy, I'm Jessica, a passionate digital artist and game designer based in South Africa. I love all things creative, and i'm akways eager to learn new skills and take on new challenges. My interests lie in creating engaging digital experiences, be it through animation, games or interactive media. Through my studies, I discovered a love for both playing and creating analogue games. I don't know the specific direction I want to take in my career, but I aspire to become a versatile artist/designer. I love all things horror, and strive to oneday create a horror game unlike anything else out there. A game so terrifying that it becomes a cult classic, and maybe even the next Resident Evil... My ultimate goal is to create a horror game of my own that Markiplier plays. I love all things vintage, and am a sucker for era's gone by. My interests span from all things Western to the 80s and 90s. I have a particular love for vintage fashion, and often find myself thrifting for unique pieces to add to my wardrobe. Growing up, I have always been surrounded by motorcycles, and ridden them since I was 4 years old. I love classic cars and bikes and also the style of traditional tattoos.
