Matey

  • Genre: Dating Sim / Visual Novel
  • Status: Final Touches
  • Date: 2025

    • This Dating Sim/Visual Novel was made in my 2nd year Game Design course. The game was made in Unity with the help of Inky. You play as a pirate captain who finds themselves in possession of a new crew and ship, following an unfortunate encounter with the almighty Kraken. Get to know your new crew and find a new 1st mate to sail with you on your next adventure. I was responsible for the art and design for this game. My contributions included concept art, character design, asset creation and UI design and implementation.

    Pandora's Box

  • Genre: Platformer
  • Status: Completed
  • Date: 2026

    • An arcade inspired platformer made for the 2026 Arcademia Game Jam. The theme was "Pandora's Box". This game was conceptualised and developed over the course of 48 hours. This game was made by a team of 5, with myself and another member working on the art and design. This game placed worldwide in the top 3 for the jam, and won the category for most arcade-like game. Play as Pandora, as she navigates through the various levels of the underworld in an attemot to retrieve the sins she released from the box.
